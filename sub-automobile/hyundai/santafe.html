SANTA FE


The Hyundai Santa fe is a sport utility vehicle (SUV) produced by the South Korean manufacturer Hyundai since 2000. Santa fe is a luxurious vehicle which falls in the category of expensive vehicle here in Nepal. Santa fe is a 2200 cc engine car which produces higher power than any average vehicle runnng in the highway of Nepal. Santa fe looks robust and stylish from front to back. There is plenty of space and superior luggage capicity.
