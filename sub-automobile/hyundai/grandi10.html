GRAND i10


Grand i10 falls under the hatch back model. The Petrol engine is 1197 cc. It is available with the Manual transmission. Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Grand i10 has a mileage of 18.9 kmpl. The Grand i10 is a 5 seater Hatchback and has a length of 3765mm, width of 1660mm and a wheelbase of 2425mm.Consisting of 5-speed gear box this vehicle is best for daily usage and even preferable for long drives.
