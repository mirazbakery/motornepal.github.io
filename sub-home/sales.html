Motor Sales in Nepal


Nepal Automobile Dealer Association(NADA) is the only one national level organization of automobile dealers. This association is responsible for every action in automobile industry here in Nepal.

There are various distributors and dealers that involve in sales of vehicles imported from various countries in Nepal. Onbasisof the vehicles and their distribution there are numerous automobile distributors in Nepal.
